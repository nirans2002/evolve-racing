What is Evolve Racing ?

Evolve Racing is a group of passionate engineering students who have joined together to design and develop a fully functional Electric Vehicle powered by solar energy. The motto of our Club is to integrate our acquired engineering knowledge into developing a sustainable solution to the existing problem of over dependency on depleting energy reserves. Through the careful design curation by various subsystems, we ought to represent our dream vehicle in the Electric Solar Vehicle Competition conducted by ESVC.
